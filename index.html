How to Make Homemade Pasta

Making pasta from scratch is a delightful and rewarding experience. It connects us to tradition and gives us control over ingredients.

Homemade pasta is rich in nutrients like Vitamin B12 and Iron. A 100g portion contains about 131 calories1.

Below you'll find all the information you need to make delicious pasta at home — from ingredients to cooking time. Don’t skip the resting step — it makes the dough easier to roll!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Make a mound of flour on a clean surface and create a well in the center.
  2. Crack the eggs into the well and add the olive oil and salt.
  3. Use a fork to beat the eggs and gradually incorporate flour until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Wrap in plastic wrap and let rest for 30 minutes.
  6. Roll out and cut into desired shape. Cook in boiling salted water for 2–4 minutes.
